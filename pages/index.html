Who are we?

Toastmc is a development group organised and owned by RemainingToast. Most of the team came from and continue to maintain Toast Client as the Minecraft Servers are all solely owned by RemainingToast, therefore members of Toastmc are only developers for the Toastmc GitHub organisation.

You can join the official discord in order to keep up with development news. NOTE: This is the discord for the development side of toastmc, you can find the anarchy server discord here
